# System Card

## System Name

HR Policy Assistant RAG

## Purpose

Answer general HR policy questions for employees using approved HR policy documents.

## Users

Employees, managers, HR partners, HR leadership, Legal.

## Capabilities

- Answer PTO and benefits questions
- Explain parental leave policy
- Explain promotion criteria
- Explain remote work policy
- Refuse restricted employee-specific requests

## Data Sources

- General HR policies
- Restricted compensation records
- Restricted medical records
- Restricted investigation records
- Legal hold records
- Performance improvement records

## Actions

The system only answers questions. It does not make employment decisions or update HR systems.

## Limitations

- Cannot verify facts outside the knowledge base
- Cannot provide legal or medical advice
- Must not disclose restricted records
- Must cite sources when answering policy questions

## Primary Risks

- Confidential data disclosure
- Medical privacy violation
- Compensation leakage
- Investigation leakage
- Hallucinated HR policy
- Prompt injection
