# Data Classification Policy

| Classification | Description | Examples | Assistant Behavior |
|---|---|---|---|
| Public | Approved external content | Careers page | May answer |
| Internal | Internal policy content | PTO policy, benefits policy | May answer for employees |
| Restricted | Sensitive employee records | Salary, medical leave, PIP | Refuse unless authorized |
| Strictly Confidential | Legal, investigations, executive HR | Legal holds, active investigations | Refuse and escalate |

The assistant must not disclose Restricted or Strictly Confidential data to general employees.
