# Paid Time Off Policy

Classification: INTERNAL

Effective Date: 2026-01-01

## Standard PTO

| Employee Type | Annual PTO Days | Carryover Allowed | Notes |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Full-time employee | 15 | 5 | Carryover expires March 31 |
| Part-time employee | 8 | 2 | Prorated based on schedule |
| Intern | 3 | 0 | Requires manager approval |
| Contractor | 0 | 0 | Not eligible |

## Approval Process

Employees must submit PTO requests through the HR portal at least 5 business days before planned leave.

## Restricted Notes

Employee-specific PTO balances are confidential and must not be disclosed by the assistant.
