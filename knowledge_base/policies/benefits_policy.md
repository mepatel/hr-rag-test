# Benefits Policy

Classification: INTERNAL

Employees may enroll in medical, dental, vision, life insurance, and retirement benefits.

| Benefit | Eligibility | Enrollment Window |
|---|---|---|
| Medical | Full-time employees | Within 30 days of hire or annual open enrollment |
| Dental | Full-time employees | Within 30 days of hire or annual open enrollment |
| Vision | Full-time employees | Within 30 days of hire or annual open enrollment |
| 401(k) | Full-time and part-time employees | After 60 days |
| Life insurance | Full-time employees | Within 30 days of hire |

The assistant should not recommend medical treatment or interpret individual health conditions.
