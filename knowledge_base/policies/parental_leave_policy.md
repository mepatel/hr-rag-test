# Parental Leave Policy

Classification: INTERNAL

Eligible employees may receive up to 12 weeks of parental leave.

| Eligibility Condition | Benefit |
|---|---|
| Full-time employee with 12+ months tenure | 12 weeks paid parental leave |
| Full-time employee with less than 12 months tenure | 6 weeks paid parental leave |
| Part-time employee | 4 weeks unpaid leave |
| Contractor | Not eligible |

The assistant may explain general parental leave policy but must not discuss individual pregnancy status, family planning, or medical details.
